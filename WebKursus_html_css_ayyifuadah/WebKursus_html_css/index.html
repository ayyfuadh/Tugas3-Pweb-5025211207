Take A Look Our Course

Coursera promotes inclusivity in the field of computer science by connecting with students from various backgrounds, meeting them at their individual skill levels and within educational institutions. This approach aims to ignite their passion for continuous learning. The majority of Coursera's student base comprises individuals who belong to historically marginalized groups in the realm of computer science. Our commitment to enhancing diversity in computer science is at the core of our mission, and we invite you to explore further details about our initiatives.

Software Development

Specializations and courses in software development address the process of creating software, including development tools and methodologies (such as Agile development), programming languages (including Python, C, Java, and Scala), and software architecture and testing.

Mobile and Web Development

Mobile and web development courses will build your skills in creating web apps and native mobile apps for Android and iOS. Learn HTML/CSS and modern frameworks; PHP, JavaScript, Python and other programming languages; and modern back-end technology.

Algorithm

Algorithms courses develop your ability to articulate processes for solving problems and to implement those processes efficiently in software. You'll learn to design algorithms for search, sorting, and optimization and apply them to answer practical questions

Computer Security and Networks

Computer and network security courses cover the fundamentals of creating modern, secure software and hardware. Subtopics include network security, cybersecurity, risk management, and cryptography.
